How the claim works

From pull to prize in four steps

Simple and human — no app and no account required to submit your claim.

1

Start your claim

Use the winner insert that came tucked in with your pack — it brings you right here to the claim form.

2

Submit the claim

Add your name, contact, and where to send the prize. Takes about a minute.

3

We verify by hand

We review your claim against our records to make sure it's a real, unclaimed winner.

4

We get it to you

Your authentic graded card ships insured & tracked — or we arrange a local handoff if you're nearby.

What to have ready

Have these on hand before you start so your claim goes through smoothly:

  • Your winner insert & the pack it came with. Keep the two together — hold onto the insert that was tucked in with your pack.
  • Roughly when & where you pulled it. An approximate date, plus the spot or venue where you found the machine.
  • Your contact details. A name and an email or phone number so we can reach you to arrange your prize.
  • A photo (optional). A quick photo of your pack and insert helps us confirm faster. See the note under the form for how to send it.
