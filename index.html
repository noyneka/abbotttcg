What's inside

One pull. One flat price. Every pack a mystery.

The Card Stop sells one thing: a mystery Pokémon booster pack. Pick a slot and the machine drops a random, factory-sealed, authentic Pokémon booster pack in a protective case. You don't pick the set — every pull is a surprise. Card payment only — no cash needed.

MYSTERY BOOSTER PACK · CASED

Mystery Pokémon booster pack — $25

Pick a slot and the machine drops a random, factory-sealed Pokémon booster pack in a protective top-loader case. You won't know which pack — or which set — until it lands. That's the fun: every pack is 100% authentic and sealed the moment it drops, plus a chance to win a graded chase card.

How to play

  1. Tap or insert your card — $25 flat.
  2. Pick any slot.
  3. Your cased mystery Pokémon booster pack drops to the tray.
